You are welcome here

Dieppe is known for its residents’ strength, spirit of cooperation and vibrancy. The city’s many services contribute to a quality of life sought out by a growing number of young families seeking a community which is stable, comfortable, safe and productive. Even through significant demographic growth, we have succeeded in maintaining a community spirit which our residents appreciate.

Many activities for all age groups are offered throughout the year. Our parks and recreational facilities are the envy of many.
